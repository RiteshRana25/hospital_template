Eligibility Criteria

The COVID-19 vaccination is available for individuals who meet the following criteria:

  • Age 18 and above.

  • No history of severe allergic reactions to vaccines.

  • Not currently pregnant or breastfeeding (consult with your healthcare provider).

  • No history of COVID-19 infection in the past 10 days.

  • No current symptoms of COVID-19, such as fever, cough, or shortness of breath.

  • Not currently under isolation or quarantine orders.

  • No recent exposure to someone with confirmed COVID-19 within the past 14 days.

  • No history of adverse reactions to previous doses of the COVID-19 vaccine.

Please consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice based on your health condition.
